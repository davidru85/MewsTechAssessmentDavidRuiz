# Smart Guest Management Project

This document covers the features and details of a system that lets guests handle their own stay, putting room controls, chat with the hotel, and booking extra services all in one place.

### Room Controls & Automation

- **AC & Heating:** Full control over the room's climate control/AC system.

- **Lighting:** Being able to turn the room lights on and off.

- **Blinds/Shades:** An automatic system to open and close the blinds.

- **Keyless Entry:** Unlocking the room door just by bringing the guest's phone close to it.

### Security & Access Control

- **Timed Access Permissions:** A system that automatically cuts off room control once the guest's checkout time hits.

- **Staff Tracking:** An access system (using cards, apps, etc.) that requires cleaning staff or other employees to log in and identify themselves before opening the door.

### Notifications & Alerts

- **Away-from-Room Monitoring:** Sending alerts to guests if anything happens in their room while they're out (like when housekeeping walks in).

- **Checkout Reminder:** An automated message sent the day before departure to remind guests of the checkout time.

### Chat & Hotel Services

- **Front Desk Requests:** A dedicated section to order room service, breakfast, or ask for extra towels and amenities.

- **Add-ons & Extras:** A quick way to book extra hotel perks, like reserving a parking spot, arranging luggage storage, or hailing a cab.

### General Info & Profile

- **General Hotel Info:** Helpful details about the hotel facilities, like pool hours, dining times, and gym schedules.

- **Guest Profile:** A private area where guests can double-check their details (name, ID, reservation info).
