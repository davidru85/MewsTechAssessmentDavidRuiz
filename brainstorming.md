# Simple Room Control Idea

Here is the basic concept in plain English:

- **What is the problem?** Guests get annoyed by confusing wall switches, and hotels lose money by heating empty rooms.
- **How do we know people want it?** We look at energy bills, ask staff about common complaints, and put QR codes in rooms to see if guests try to scan them.
- **What are we building for the demo?** A clean, simple phone app to let guests toggle lights and change the temp, using simulated room responses on the phone.
- **How do we make it real later?** We structure the app code so we can easily swap the simulated controls for real hotel hardware connections down the road.
- **How do we get guests to use it?** Suggest it when they join the hotel Wi-Fi or tap their keycard. We measure success by how much energy is saved.
- **How do we do this alone?** Focus only on energy-saving features, use standard pre-made layouts to make it look nice, and drop anything too complicated.
- **Where does AI help?** Having AI write the starting files and make fake data to show how it works.
